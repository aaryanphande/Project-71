Curriculum

Our curriculum revolves around the multi-level play, activity based learning, discovery inter active classroom and experiential learning in the sciences, mathematics, arts, social sciences and humanities. The School curriculum offers an exciting and challenging range of subjects designed to develop a strong foundation of skills and knowledge. We aim to nurture a love of learning and give pupils the confidence to be fully involved in all aspects of their education. Policy

The School Year

The school follows the annual calendar of the Department of Education. The school session begins in April and closes in March. Summer vacation is usually in the months of May and June. Short breaks are provided around Diwali, Ganesh Chaturthi, Christmas and New Year. Dates for vacations vary each year and must be verified from the school almanac. Gazetted holidays are followed as per government rules. Details about school events are also listed in the school almanac.

Books,Stationery and Uniform

The school takes special care to select and identify books that support our educational philosophy and simultaneously equip our students to be at par with desirable scholarly levels. Stationery kits and uniform information will be published from time to time.

School Transport

The school transport facility is outsourced. The bus fee is calculated for twelve months of the year, as per the number of users each year. An official request is a must to avail the facility.

School Fees

School fee details are available at the enquiry desk. You can seek an appointment with the counselor regarding the details and clarification for the same. INGS follows NO REFUND policy. No amount in part or full, will be refunded under any circumstances.
